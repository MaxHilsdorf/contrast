A Quick Tour Through My Professional Background

In academia, I worked as a Student Research Assistant at Justus-Liebig-University (Giessen, GER), analyzing and visualizing data as well as co-authoring multiple scientific publications in the field of music psychology.

My bachelor's thesis in musicology on the interaction of personality, uses of music, and the acceptance of music streaming services was published in Frontiers in Psychology.

In my data science studies, I developed a data processing pipeline that automates all pre-machine-learning steps for a wide range of audio classification tasks. This pipeline will be published on GitHub soon.

As a Data Scientist, I currently work at Cyanite, developing AI solutions for a wide range of music companies, focusing on audio-based deep learning and NLP.

On Medium, I have published over a dozen articles in the domains of data science and musicology, accumulating to over 300,000 views.
